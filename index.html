Proactive Education

Every life event presents an educational opportunity or a “teaching moment”. At BTEC, we provide a proactive education approach to emerging technology putting our students at the forefront of each industry. Our education platform introduces students to emerging technologies, gives access to knowledge through education courses. Our platform was created to help students learn, earn and evolve.


BTEC: Blockchain Technology Education Center

Building Equity through emerging Technology.

Change is constant.

At BTEC, make blockchain technology education and the emerging technologies around it available to all communities. Through workshops, discussions, webinars, research and test lab ideas of an emerging world will be introduced and explored. Founder Jonnet Solomon saw a need for emerging technology education when consulting on a Blockchain Technology Project. The issue was not the technology, it was the fact that local communities were unaware that innovation and emerging technologies was happening. Essentially, there was no buy-in. The next step was to educate the community about Blockchain which led to introducing all Emerging Technology.
